Benefits of Starting a Chapter or Club-Chapter

  • Access Coding Tomorrow Resources, Staff and Curriculums
  • Give back to your community!
  • Differentiate yourself from your peers.
    • Help make an impact in your community.​
  • Network with employees in various tech. companies
    • Work with our corporate volunteers and get personal insights into the technology workplace!​
  • Volunteering with us provides better future job prospects
  • Gain leadership experience through taking on the task and responsibility of leading a chapter!

WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR

  • Prior leadership experience
    • Club Officer Experience
    • Project Leader Experience
    • Other forms of leadership experiences.
  • Passion for service: In order to help others, we must have the spirit for it.
  • Programming Experience: Fundamental-intermediate understanding of code is recommended, though not compulsory.
  • Initiative: Taking the lead in pushing forward, and having a creative drive in taking the helm.
