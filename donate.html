Why Coding Tomorrow Initiative?

As a student run organization, our main priority is dedicating 100% of donation funds to our operations or reserves. Our organization relies on the compassion of our volunteers in bringing digital education to technologically marginalized communities. Your donation will make a greater impact in all of our communities. Additionally, the top five donators will be shown in our donations page as a gesture of our appreciation.

Once more, we cannot thank you enough for your generous financial support, and we appreciate your willingness to invest in our future.

Our purpose is to increase technological literacy and promote important programming skills. In a world where technology is expanding to all aspects of our lives, especially our economic future, it is essential that no one is left behind. We seek to educate, enable, and build each and every one of our community members --- starting with our youths with computer knowledge. We believe that every person has a right of access to the internet and to be educated on the matters of such.
