ABOUT US

Who are we?

We are a group of students that believes every person has the right to access the internet and deserves the opportunity to a proper internet education. Our time is dedicated to the proliferation of programming education opportunities, online literacy, and knowledge of internet etiquette.

WHAT DO WE DO?

Currently, our efforts primarily consist of providing free coding classes for kids in boot camp style classes. Our programming classes curriculum includes HTML, Python, CSS, and Java, with the number of languages extending. Additionally, we are also introducing basic internet etiquette classes, in order to better introduce the online environment to those who aren't as familiar.
